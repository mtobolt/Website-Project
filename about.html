Pricing

Lym Photography custom portrait sessions are designed for you and your family: I am available to meet at a park, your home, your favorite ice cream shop, or anywhere else that is meaningful to you or your family.

Each session begins with a consultation to make sure the session is unique to you and your family and reflects the best of your family's personality. We will then have fun together until we have at least 50 edited, high-resolution images for you to view in a private online gallery. Session fees start at $220, and a number of packages are available to reflect your need.

FAQ

Q: What is your photo style?

I love simple, warm, and luminous photos, and I try to catch loving moments. You always look more beautiful in my lens. I love the innocent eyes of children, the pretty smiles of girls, the sweet anticipation of new mothers, the love between mothers and daughters, and the happiness of lovers. Free feel to visit my blog to get a better idea of my style.

Q: What happens at a photo shoot?

I like outdoor photos and natural light. I usually take photos around sunrise or sunset because that’s when the light is most golden: the light creates a warm and comfortable time for photos, and I always like sparkling hair and smiles against the light. Because of this, I would be happy to postpone your photo shoot in case of bad weather.

Q: Where are the photo shoots?

Wherever you want! We can go to the park, a forest, a cafe, your backyard, your favorite ice cream shop — anywhere that you like or anywhere that has special meaning for you.

Q: Which camera and equipment do you use?

I love Canon’s soft tone, so I use Canon 5D Mark II bodies, with mostly prime lenses: I love the 50mm/1.2L lens the best. I use the Contax 645 for film. I am in love with the color of film: tender, soft, and creamy. Meanwhile, I still have a Polaroid Camera because it is so much fun to play with!

Q: How can we prepare for a photo shoot?

Feel free to contact with me at lymphotography@gmail for specific advice for your shoot. For example, the choice of clothes is very important; I usually suggest wearing clothes that (a) match the environment that you pick, and (b) are simple, so that the clothes do not draw attention from you. The color of the clothes depends on the environment, though, so we can discuss that when you contact me.

Q: Can I bring props?

Sure! I would love that! If you have an even better idea than I do for the environment, please tell me! I love for you to have something memorable and special so that the photos will be more unique and meaningful for you.

Q: How long is a photo shoot?

It depends on which session you pick; portrait sessions run from 30 minutes to 2 hours or more, and wedding session run to 8 hours.

Q: What do we get from a photo sessions?

All photo sessions include enhanced high-resolution photos for you to keep the loving moments forever; you will also receive film prints if you select a film session. Wedding clients receive a fine print photo album in addition to enhanced high-resolution photos.

Q: How long will it take to get the photos?

The average portrait session takes approximately 2 to 3 weeks to edit and deliver, and the average wedding takes approximately 4 to 6 weeks to edit and deliver.
